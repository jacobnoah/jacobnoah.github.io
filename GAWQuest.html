GAWQuest Internship Project

This last summer I worked for a company called Gottlieb and Wertz. Their business is in charge of managing all the database records for juveniles in many counties across Indiana. One important aspect of their business is the website. I was tasked with taking in a text file filled with millions of actions performed on the website and had to sort all of the data by categories and then by types and show in heirarchical ranking. What you can see in the image above is a sample run from the month of June 2019 and Equest/dynamicui/alertmessages was performed the most at over 14 million.
